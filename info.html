About Me

Hello, my name is Masaki Nawa. I am a masters student studying Finance and Buisness Analytics at the University of Iowa. Prior to Iowa, I studied in Computer Science at Grinnell College. I am interested in Software Engineering, Visualization, and Data Science.

My coding journey started in Spring 2020, after taking my first Computer Science class in college. I enjoy problem-solving and tackling challenges while coding. I have learned different languages such as Java, C/C#, Python, R, and JS and developed several projects in the past few years. Check out the Projects tab at the top of the page to see some of my products.

Starting in May, I'll be working as a researcher for the NSF-funded statistics education project Stat2Lab, coordinated by Grinnell College Professor Shonda Kuiper. I'll be in charge of creating new Unity-based instructional games that allow students to apply statistical analysis to game tactics.

I'm a risk-taker with a strong desire to solve problems. I'm a data-driven, goal-oriented programmer who values teamwork, flexibility, and innovation. I appreciate working in an environment that is diverse, self-motivated, and challenging, with a high learning curve and an open-minded mindset.
